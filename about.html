Hi, I'm Ana - I'm a 16 year old who really enjoys doing a whole load of things, especially cyber security (forensics!) and computer science. Thanks for visiting my page, and I hope this provides a bit of insight into a few of my many interests. Check out my blog (linked above) for more!

Things I've Done!

  • Google Code-In - I was a winner of Google's Code-In contest, a competition meant to encourage 13-17 year olds into open-source development. I won for the organisation PostgreSQL in 2018.

  • Cyber - I have attended Cyber Discovery's Elite camps in both 2018 and 2019, being one of 170 selected from 23000 in 2018, and winning a SANS coin and becoming GIAC-certified in the latter year. I also attended CSC's Team UK Qualifier in March 2019, and participate in loads of CTFs with my CTF team, PWN to 0xE4. Finally, I came 2nd in CyberCenturion VI with my team, System of a Downtime.

Current Projects...

  • GitHub - You can check out my GitHub over here. I mostly write projects in Python, and write websites with HTML and CSS (like this one!), but I also can write in JS, Ruby, and C and recently learned C++. I'd like to expand to Haskell in the future.

  • Stegosaurus - As a participant in CTFs, I wanted to create a GUI tool to automate Steg and some Forensics challenges. The result of this is Stegosaurus, a Python project I'm working on in order to solve such challenges with worked solutions, featuring a user-friendly interface letting both beginners and experts use it.

  • Cyber Discovery Discord - I admin the Cyber Discovery Discord server currently, and contribute to some of its projects sometimes. I am currently building a ModLog system, allowing for admins to track rule infractions by user.
